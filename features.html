For Everyone!

Whether you're the CEO of a Fortune 500 Company, the General Manager of your local breakfast joint, a busy parent with five kids running around the house, or a college student with a never-ending list of things to do, MOP will keep your life in order!

Mop allows you to join multiple to-do lists, one for each organization, team, or project. Your dashboard will display a composite list of your tasks from all the lists you are a part of, and organize them by due date/time.
